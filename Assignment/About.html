About

Patricia Chibuzor Utulu


Hi, I am the main photographer and owner of Tricia
Photography. I am 18 years old, studying for a B.A (Hons.)
in Graphic Design. From a young age, my passion for
photography has grown more and more everyday.

Apart from my passion for photography, I also have a
passion for singing and dancing. This gives me an eye for
detail. My aim as a photographer is to give my clients the
best possible experience. I want to capture their special
moments and freeze them in time.

I look forward to working with future clients and I can’t
wait to capture your moments. To get in touch please
leave a message through our contact form and I will get
back to you as soon as possible.
